CMMS for managing local authorities and administrations

Optimize the maintenance and operation of the assets or networks of local authorities and administrations.

CARL Berger-Levrault has been designing maintenance and asset management software solutions since 1985 to meet the challenges facing towns and cities. Designed in partnership with the major actors in the field (technical and property operators, landlords, facilities departments, professional associations, etc.), our tools help you meet the new constraints of the Smart City: CARL Source City.

Optimize the maintenance and operation of the assets or networks of local authorities and administrations. No matter what your administration (ministry, trade union, etc.) or your local authority (municipality, council, region, territory, state, etc.), and no matter the nature of the assets or networks for which you are responsible (parks, gardens, buildings, plots of land, educational establishment, water, sanitation, engineering structures, major infrastructures, public lighting, signaling, roads, etc.), you need to provide citizens with the best terms and conditions of use of the resources at their disposal and ensure legal compliance, all the while optimizing costs.

Managing your assets and networks

CARL Source City helps you to structure the knowledge database of your sites throughout their life cycle

  • Each key building or infrastructure element (plot, site, building, floor, room, technical equipment, etc.) is described on an appropriate identity card: Condition record, Technical characteristics, Classifications as a ‚ÄòPublic Building‚Äô, Warranties, Multimedia documents, Surface areas, Depreciation values, Occupied plots, Geocoded routes and addresses, etc.
  • Interactive and intuitive multi-tree display of your equipment: Functional, location, technical, etc.
  • Graphic navigation, integration, and interactivity of diagrams and maps (GIS), 3D digital models (BIM), geolocation of equipment, etc.
  • Direct access to key information and to the e-record of devices: History of maintenance work, Preventive plans, Diagrams and drawings, Risks and precautions to be observed, etc.
  • Visual representation of energy networks and utilities: Immediate display of the consequences in the event of a failure occurring on a critical device.
  • Track rental contracts (leases for landlords and tenants) from initial signature and walk-through report to receipts for rent and rental charges.
  • Manage occupiers (associations, local authorities, legal entities, etc.) and optimize leased surfaces.

Guarantee equipment uptime and personnel safety

CARL Source City helps you to guarantee a high level of equipment uptime and ensures user safety

  • Help Desk functions for citizens to improve responsiveness and their enjoyment of the quality of the service rendered.
  • Analysis and assistance with troubleshooting by presenting possible solutions according to the symptoms and the identified causes of failure.
  • Take account of regulatory constraints and safety registers to facilitate legal compliance.
  • Take account of the level of certification of the person(s) involved when assigning work tasks.

Monitor your service providers and manage your contracts

CARL Source City provides the means to analyze and monitor your suppliers

  • Manage purchases and stocks: purchasing contracts, procurement contracts, supplier qualification and indicators, manufacturer lots, etc.
  • Create a single article and service repository that includes the conditions negotiated in your contracts.
  • Import supplier catalogs and price updates.
  • Issue alert in the event of non-compliance with contractual obligations.
  • Quantified and objective elements to assess the performance of service providers.
  • Run checks to verify the actual presence of service providers.
  • Direct access to service providers verified as per the scope of current contracts.

What is enterprise asset management?

Enterprise asset management centralises and streamlines the lifecycle of physical assets, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and reliability for critical infrastructure.

How does CARL integrate with other systems?

CARL connects with SCADA, BMS, GIS, ERP, and IoT platforms for unified data, automated workflows, and real-time operational visibility.

Who benefits from using CARL?

Organisations in utilities, airports, ports, government, energy, mining, transport, and manufacturing with complex asset networks benefit from CARL.

What mobile capabilities are available?

The CARL Touch app enables technicians to access asset data, complete checklists, and work offline for efficient field operations.

How is data migration handled?

We securely migrate asset and maintenance data from legacy systems or spreadsheets, ensuring a seamless transition with minimal disruption.

What support is offered post-implementation?

Ongoing support, training, and system optimisation are provided to ensure continuous improvement and long-term value.
