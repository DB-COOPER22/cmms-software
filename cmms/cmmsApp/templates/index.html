FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Get the answers you need

Explore our solutions for asset management, digital transformation, and the CARL platform.

What is enterprise asset management?

Enterprise asset management centralises and streamlines the lifecycle of physical assets, ensuring compliance, efficiency, and reliability for critical infrastructure.

How does CARL integrate with other systems?

CARL connects with SCADA, BMS, GIS, ERP, and IoT platforms for unified data, automated workflows, and real-time operational visibility.

Who benefits from using CARL?

Organisations in utilities, airports, ports, government, energy, mining, transport, and manufacturing with complex asset networks benefit from CARL.

What mobile capabilities are available?

The CARL Touch app enables technicians to access asset data, complete checklists, and work offline for efficient field operations.

How is data migration handled?

We securely migrate asset and maintenance data from legacy systems or spreadsheets, ensuring a seamless transition with minimal disruption.

What support is offered post-implementation?

Ongoing support, training, and system optimisation are provided to ensure continuous improvement and long-term value.
