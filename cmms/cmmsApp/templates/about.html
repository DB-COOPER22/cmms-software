About Us

About us – where vision meets execution!

iEngineering Australia is a technology-driven solutions provider specializing in engineering software, industrial automation, and digital transformation services. We partner with organizations across multiple industries to deliver innovative, scalable, and cost-effective solutions tailored to their operational needs. With a strong focus on reliability, customer-centricity, and sustainability, our mission is to empower businesses with tools that enhance efficiency, improve asset performance, and drive growth.

Our Journey

Founded in 2017, iEngineering Australia has rapidly grown into a trusted technology partner delivering innovative solutions in asset management, IoT, cloud platforms, and digital transformation. From our beginnings as a small engineering services provider, we have expanded our expertise across industries such as utilities, transport, energy, manufacturing, and public infrastructure. Today, we are proud to support organizations across Australia and the Asia-Pacific region with smart, scalable, and future-ready technology solutions.
