CARL Work Plans

Plan, schedule, and execute preventive maintenance with confidence—backed by real-time data, maps, and mobile execution.

Request a demo Get pricing

CARL Work Plans is the planning engine inside CARL Source that turns maintenance policy into actionable, scheduled work. Build time-, meter-, and condition-based plans, simulate workloads, reserve skills and parts, and publish optimized technician calendars—then execute via CARL Touch on Android/iOS with full traceability. Add CARL Optim (AI scheduling), CARL Maps (2D/3D plans & GIS), and CARL IoT (sensor triggers) to go from reactive to predictive.

What’s in CARL Work Plans

Plan Design & Automation

Build time-, meter-, and condition-based plans to automate your maintenance strategy.

  • Plan types: calendar-time, usage-based, and condition/IoT-driven thresholds.
  • Templates & tasks: reusable job plans with labor, tools, authorizations, and checklists.
  • Auto-generation of work orders: plans release WOs automatically with assignment rules.

Scheduling & Capacity

Manage workloads and optimize schedules to maximize crew efficiency.

  • Graphical planning: drag, drop, and adjust schedules inside CARL Source.
  • AI optimization: propose the best resource schedule considering skills, availability, and SLAs.
  • Workload simulation: preview plan releases and smooth peaks before publishing.

Spatial Context (optional: CARL Maps)

Visualize assets and scheduled work on maps, floor plans, and BIM models.

  • See assets on 2D/3D maps, plans, BIM, and navigate from site maps down to rooms.
  • Launch work, inspections, and searches directly from the map; geoposition equipment.

Execution on Mobile (CARL Touch)

Empower technicians to complete work orders in the field with a powerful mobile app.

  • Technicians receive work with priorities & checklists, capture photos, meters, and parts.
  • Capture client e-signatures, online or offline.
