Elevator Pitch

CARL Mobility is the mobile suite for CARL Source that equips every role—from requesters to technicians and stock managers—with fast, intuitive apps. Create and track requests, receive and complete work orders, capture photos and signatures, manage parts, and sync data in real time or offline. Built for Android and iOS, CARL Mobility accelerates response times, boosts first-time fix rates, and gives you auditable maintenance records end-to-end.

Who it's for

Technicians & Crews

Receive work orders, checklists, meters, capture photos/notes, log time, and collect e-signatures.

Stock Managers

Perform receipts, transfers, and inventory counts on the go.

All Employees & Tenants

Submit service requests with photos/voice and track status via CARL Flash.

Key Outcomes

  • Faster interventions & higher first-time fix with real-time assignments, guided reports, and checklists.
  • Full traceability—digital reports, timestamps, signatures, and geo-context.
  • Lower inventory costs via mobile receipts, issues, and cycle counts.
  • Fewer duplicate requests with geo-tagged, deduplicated service tickets.

The CARL Mobility Apps

CARL Touch

For Technicians & Stores

  • Work orders in your pocket: see tasks, SLAs, priorities, and new assignments in real time.
  • Smart reporting: complete digital reports, add spare parts, time, and photos; capture customer e-signatures.
  • Hardware power: scan barcodes/QR, use camera, GPS, NFC, and more; Android & iOS compatible.

CARL Flash

For Requesters & Tenants

  • Create requests in seconds: with wizards, photos/voice, barcode scan, and geolocation.
  • Avoid duplicates & spot emergencies: when multiple users report the same issue.
  • Track status: from submission to resolution—no calls required.

Works anywhere—even with poor connectivity

Keep working offline—create tasks, log activities, add parts and counters; data syncs when back online. Ideal for plants, remote sites, and basements.

Go beyond mobile: spatial & IoT intelligence (optional add-ons)

CARL Maps

See assets on 2D/3D maps, plans, BIM, and navigate from site maps down to rooms. Launch work, inspections, and searches directly from the map; geoposition equipment.

BL.Predict / CARL IoT

Connect sensors and equipment data to predict failures, trigger preventive work, and improve uptime.
